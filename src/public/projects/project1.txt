A simple FPS game where enemies follow you and they need to be shot down. A pistol, uzi and shotgun are the weapons available as of now. 
There are 2 kinds of enemies, Bombs and Robots, Bombs deal burst damage whereas Robots deal normal damage.
Developed using the Unity game engine in C#
